**Pianpker** is a static blog theme built with [Astro](https://astro.build/).

It treats wabi-sabi, yūgen, mono no aware, and *ma* as principles of typography: a warm paper-like ground, measured layers of ink, restrained vermilion accents, and whitespace that lets language breathe. The form stays as simple as possible so the content can remain complex; every visible element should have a reason to exist.

**Less, between the lines.**

Pianpker is built with [Astro](https://astro.build/) and extensively customized from [Retypeset](https://github.com/radishzzz/astro-theme-retypeset).
